**creativity**

ability to produce something new and useful, depends on:
* intelligence --> not too much, not too little
* knowledge
* personality --> willing to overcome and take risks
* motivation
    * implicit --> success etc
    * explicit --> money
* environment
* lateral thinking

**inventor**

* master of their field but not only
* more interested in problems
* never take everything as true
* seek global solutions

**basic research**

increasing the understanding of a topic (science driven)

**applied research**

increasing the understanding of a topic to meet a specific need (demeand driven)

**development**

activity that from knowledge produce something useful
